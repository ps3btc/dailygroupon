Ever wondered what the hottest deals on Groupon are? Or what Groupon's daily revenue is? On December 27, 2010, before heading out for dinner (ethiopian) I asked myself these questions. Almost immediately, I learned that Groupon exposed an API. So obviously, I had to hack something up.

I estimate revenue by multiplying the quantity sold and the price of a given deal. I only do this if the deal has tipped, meaning, the deal is on.

Everyone on the interwebs seems to speculate what Groupon's revenue is. Hello! They have an API, we can chart this already :)

As you can see, I have no UI skills. I built this website in under 6 hours. If you want to extend this project, drop me a note at hareesh.nagarajan AT gmail.

Groupon appears to sell deals in US Dollars (USD) and in Canadian Dollars (CAD). The revenue in CAD is a tiny number. Since 1 CAD ~ 1 USD, these days, I've converted all revenue to USD.

Update Jan 4, 2010: I noticed that Groupon has a number of deals that span multiple days. So before 1/4/2010, I was carrying over the revenue for multi day deal. I decided to fix that. So if a deal is on for 3 days, and it nets in $200 in revenue by day 2, then I assign $200/3 as revenue for that deal on day 2.

Of course, I'm in no way affiliated with Groupon. I simply built this serivce to have some fun. All possible shortcuts were taken to code this up. The code is gross. Enjoy!
